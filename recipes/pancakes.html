Pancakes

Description

Delicious recipe of rolled pancakes made with home ingredients

Recipe is very simple, creating pastry takes less than half an hour and then you just have to fry them on pan.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients, add flour slowly and check if texture is okay
  2. Fry on the pan half of soup spoon of pastry for one pancake
  3. Decorate it with watever you like, done!
