Description

Very simple recipe of wrap with weenie instead of chicken or ham for filler

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Slice of cheese
  • 2 weenies
  • 4 green olives or 6 black olives

Steps

  1. Heat weenies on pan or electric grill
  2. Put on tortilla cheese and warmed up weenies
  3. Cut each olive into 3 or 4 slices and put them on tortilla
  4. Wrap it all and heat on pan or electric grill until tortilla is slightly brown
  5. Done!
